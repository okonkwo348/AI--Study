    Artificial Intelligence is the branch of computer science that focuses on creating machines or software that can **perform tasks that normally require human intelligence**. This includes learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, and even understanding language.

    **Key Types of AI:**

    1. **Narrow AI (Weak AI):**

    * Designed to perform a **specific task**.
    * Examples: Chatbots, recommendation systems, facial recognition.

    2. **General AI (Strong AI):**

    * Hypothetical AI that can **perform any intellectual task a human can**.
    * Still largely theoretical.

    3. **Superintelligent AI:**

    * AI that surpasses human intelligence across all domains.
    * This is speculative and a topic of ethics and safety discussions.

    **Common AI Techniques:**

    * **Machine Learning (ML):** Computers learn from data to make predictions or decisions.
    * **Deep Learning (DL):** A subset of ML using neural networks to model complex patterns.
    * **Natural Language Processing (NLP):** Enables machines to understand and generate human language.
    * **Computer Vision:** Helps machines “see” and interpret images or video.

    **Applications of AI:**

    * Virtual assistants like Siri or Alexa
    * Autonomous vehicles
    * Fraud detection in banking
    * Personalized recommendations (Netflix, YouTube, Amazon)
    * Medical diagnostics

    **Why AI Matters:**
    AI can automate tasks, analyze huge datasets, uncover patterns humans might miss, and even help solve complex problems in science, healthcare, and industry.
